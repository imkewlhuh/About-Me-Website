My Little One

This little boy is Mylo, the newest addition to the family.
Mylo was a stray found on the streets of Lower Manhattan only a few days ago.
He was small, scared, and sick. As we nursed him back to health, his belly grew.
He often found comfort laying on my belly. We are now belly bros.
I decided to keep him.
